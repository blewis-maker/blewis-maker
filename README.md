### Brandan Lewis

I build agentic systems and research **agentic swarms** — how teams of AI agents coordinate, remember, govern their own actions, and improve over time. The shift from a single assistant to a *governed fleet* that works like an organization is what I spend my time on, across three products.

#### What I'm building

- **[Agix](https://agix-ai.io)** — an operating system for a governed, legible fleet of AI agents you own and run yourself. Its open-source core, **[Agix AOS](https://github.com/blewis-maker/agix-aos-cli)**, installs a local knowledge fabric and a team of specialist agents on your own machine — no account, and no data leaves your computer (`brew install agix-aos`).
- **[Postii](https://postii.io)** — an agentic social-media marketing platform: putting a fleet of agents to work planning and running social marketing.
- **DeepSync** — an enterprise coordination, visualization, and intelligence platform, and the first canonical adopter of the architecture approach that Agix generalizes.

#### Currently

Building a **proving ground** for the bet behind the swarm: that a trained, cheap-model hive with compounding memory can match a single frontier model on quality while winning decisively on **cost and speed — and pulling ahead over time** as it learns. The whole point is to *measure* that, reproducibly and honestly about what's still a proxy, instead of asserting it.
